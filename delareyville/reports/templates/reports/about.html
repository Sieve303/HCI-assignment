About Us

This system helps residents report service delivery issues easily.

Through this platform, community members can quickly report problems such as water leaks, electricity faults, potholes, and waste collection issues.

Our goal is to improve the quality of service delivery in Delareyville by enabling efficient communication between residents and municipal services.
